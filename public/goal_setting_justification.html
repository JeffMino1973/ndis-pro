Behaviour Support & Emotional Regulation
NDIS: Social, Community & Relationships
My Goal
"I want to develop strategies to manage my anxiety and phobias so that I can interact safely with others and participate in my community without distress. I need to learn safe ways to communicate my feelings so that I do not become physically aggressive toward my family and carers."
Justified Supports Required
✓Specialist Behaviour Intervention: Comprehensive Behaviour Support Plan (BSP) to systematically address perseveration and escalation toward physical violence.
✓Therapeutic Supports (Psychologist): Regular 1:1 therapy targeting severe anxiety and phobias (focused on hygiene markers, medical supplies like bandaids, illness).
✓De-escalation Training: Specialised coaching for her primary carer (mother, aged 70) to safely defuse physical escalation.
✓Visual/Visual Scripting Tools: Developing a "safety script" to communicate distress safely as an alternative to physical aggression.
✓Functional Behaviour Assessment (FBA): Necessary to map internal and external sensory triggers causing physical outbursts.
