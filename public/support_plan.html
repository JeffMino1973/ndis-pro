8. Sunday Shopping Program
StepActivitySupport Approach
1 Plan the shopping list with Bronwyn's mother. Encourage Bronwyn to contribute items, confirm priorities and understand the purpose of the shopping trip.
2 Enter the shopping list into Bronwyn's phone. Use Apple Notes or a preferred list app so Bronwyn can follow the list during shopping.
3 Locate and select groceries. Support Bronwyn to navigate aisles, compare products and select the correct grocery items.
4 Pay for groceries. Support Bronwyn to participate in checkout routines, payment and receipt collection as appropriate.
5 Review the activity. Discuss what went well, what was difficult and what can be practised again next week.
